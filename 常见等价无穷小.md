### 常见的等价无穷小

当$x\to0$时，

$sin(x)\sim x$

$arcsin(x) \sim x$

$tan(x) \sim x$

$arctan(x) \sim x$

$ln(1+x) \sim x$

$\log_a(1+x) \sim \frac{x}{lna}$

$a^x-1 \sim x\ln a$

$e^x-1 \sim x$

$(1+x)^a-1 \sim ax$

$1-cos(x) \sim \frac{1}{2}x^2$

$tan(x)-sin(x) \sim \frac{1}{2}x^3$

$tan(x)-x \sim \frac{1}{3}x^3$

$x-sin(x) \sim \frac{1}{6}x^3$

$\sqrt[n]{1+x}-1 \sim \frac{x}{n}$
